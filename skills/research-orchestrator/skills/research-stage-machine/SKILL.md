# Research Stage Machine

Use this skill whenever active research is underway or when the answer requires a visible conservative research process instead of a direct unsupported answer.

## Visible Workflow

Use these visible stage families in plain research language:

- research question refinement
- concept and scope definition
- prior-art review
- evidence appraisal
- competing explanation comparison
- argument formation
- section drafting
- integration and revision
- final completion readiness

## Core Laws

- use visible workflow language conservatively
- per visible turn, stay or move one adjacent step
- do not skip visible steps
- let holds happen naturally when sufficiency is not met
- regress when insufficiency, contradiction, scope drift, unsupported writing, missing accountability, or archive weakness reopen earlier work
- record explicit advancement or regression rationale in durable artifacts instead of leaving only terse labels
- do not treat a few bullets or a short run-state summary as a sufficient reasoning artifact
- once section drafting is honest, the visible body must say that manuscript writing is now available or active rather than behaving like static status reporting
- prefer conservative maturity language such as stabilized, narrowed, newly opened, and not yet ready over celebratory completion language when that better matches the evidence
- do not narrate archive bootstrapping, contract checks, or skill reads in the visible body unless the user explicitly asked for a system audit

## Reporting Rule

During active research, every substantive response should open as a plain-language research progress report, not as a system panel. The visible response should cover:

- what was examined
- what was learned
- what has stabilized through prior turns when that helps orient the user
- what this turn narrowed or newly opened when that changed
- why the current research position is justified
- what remains insufficient or conflicted
- how the argument or writing readiness changed
- what next focused move is justified
- the latest cumulative ZIP path or the exact packaging boundary

Do not use raw engine tokens, fake percentages, status tables, or debug-field labels in the public body.

## Question-Gate Rule

When an ordinary-looking question honestly requires substantive research, do not answer the substance first. Start with a short research-setup note, narrow the question honestly, and open the workflow before moving into substantive evidence collection or synthesis.

## Stop Rule

Treat visible workflow language as a trust surface. If the visible position would overstate certainty or progress, step back to the most honest earlier position instead.
