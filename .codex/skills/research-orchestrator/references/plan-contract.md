Plan Contract

Use the plan-first gate for any coding request that can lead to implementation, editing, repair, or writable execution and is not already explicitly covered by the active approved plan record.

Required order:

1. short understanding report
2. explicit approval request
3. wait for approval
4. write or refresh the approved English PLAN under repo-root `plan/`
5. only then continue into writable work

Tiny follow-up edits and repair steps are not exempt unless they are already explicitly covered by the active approved plan record and keep the approved direction materially unchanged.
